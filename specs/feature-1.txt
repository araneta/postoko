Phase 1: Core Revenue Features (Weeks 1-4)
1. Barcode Scanning (HIGH PRIORITY) [done]
Why first: Dramatically speeds up checkout process
Customer impact: 3x faster transactions, reduced errors
Implementation: Use device camera to scan product barcodes
Revenue justification: Essential for any serious retail business
2. Multiple Payment Methods (HIGH PRIORITY) [working]
Why essential: Cash-only severely limits customer base
Add: Credit/debit card processing, digital wallets
Integration: Stripe or Square for payment processing
Revenue impact: Increases average transaction size by 20-30%
3. Low Stock Alerts (MEDIUM PRIORITY)
Why valuable: Prevents lost sales from stockouts
Implementation: Automatic notifications when inventory drops below threshold
Customer pain point: Manual inventory tracking is time-consuming
